WHAT IS IT?

Native Strength is a centuries-old inter-tribal traditional mental health care system.

It uses medicine wheels, teaching triangles, and traditional stories to illustrate concepts and provide useful tools for personal insite and coping strategies.

Hyapatia Lee is the founder of Native Strength, an alternative approach to the contemporary mental health care system based on ancient wisdom. This path to happiness and personal evolvement is based on a centuries-old, inter-tribal system given to us by the Star People. Information is shared with the help of medicine wheels, which are much like the circles that the aliens in the movie ARRIVAL used to communicate with humans.

Hyapatia began her research with medicine men and women across North America in the mid-1980's and expanded it to include Australia, New Zealand, and Hawaii. She incorporated what she had learned to help the people of The Lost River Band of the Cherokees in Mitchell, Indiana as their Blessed Woman for several years.

This teaching platform is the best way to learn about the Native Strength mental health and mindfulness system. Udemy makes online learning easy with videos, short quizzes, and insightful exercises available at any time of day or night.
